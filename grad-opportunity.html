Graduation Opportunities

International Mobility Opportunities

By studying at ICMC, you will have access to numerous international mobility opportunities, both through agreements that ICMC has with various foreign universities and through international mobility programs such as Ciência sem Fronteiras.

Undergraduate Research

Even during your undergraduate studies, you can begin conducting research through an undergraduate research program and, as a result, obtain scholarships that will help you financially.

Junior Enterprise

Do you want to work in your field and put all the knowledge you're acquiring into practice? Every semester you can participate in the selection process to join ICMC Júnior, a company that has been in the job market for over 20 years and is made up exclusively of students from the Institute.

Extension Programs

There are countless opportunities for you to develop extracurricular activities during your course, participating in groups such as Warthog Robotics, Fellowship of the Game (FoG), the Programming Marathon Study Group (GEMA), and the Tutorial Education Program (PET).

Internship

To bring studies closer to the job market, ICMC maintains partnerships with companies and public institutions, where you can do an internship or even be hired regularly.

Master's pathway

Being in the final year of some courses, you will have the opportunity to attend postgraduate courses and thus obtain a master's degree in just one year after graduation.
